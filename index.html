Argentina

The Argentina National Team is the most winner in the CONMEBOL Copa América. With 15 titles in her history, the albiceleste always makes her fans vibrate, as she is always a candidate to win the continental tournament.

In addition to the 15 trophies achieved, the Argentines have expressive and historical marks in the tournament. They are the biggest scorers with a total of 462 goals scored and have the biggest win with a 12-0 against Ecuador, in 1942. In the entire history of the tournament, the ‘gauchos’ have 122 victories, more than any other team, and only 33 defeats, the best selection in this section. The country also hosted the competition nine times. Returning to host the competition is a great incentive for Messi and company to bring joy to the Argentine team again.
